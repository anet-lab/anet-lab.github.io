Welcome to the ANET Lab at PTIT

Our research interests focus on pioneering the architecture of next-generation networking systems to achieve unprecedented performance, robust reliability, and seamless scalability for future communication infrastructures.

The research directions include the design and optimization of advanced network architectures, intelligent and autonomous network management, AI-driven monitoring and analytics, and zero-touch orchestration for self-configuring and self-healing networks.

We also explore the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to enable adaptive, efficient, and fully autonomous networking systems capable of supporting emerging large-scale and data-intensive applications.
